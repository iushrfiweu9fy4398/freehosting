Frequently Asked Questions

Is this hosting really free forever?

+

Yes! As a non-profit project, our mission is to provide free resources to the community. Our core free hosting package will remain free forever. We are sustained by free service provider like iFastNet (Hosting provider), Render (Web service provider) and CloudFlare (Anti-ddos and DNS management).

What are the limitations?

+

Our free plan is incredibly generous but is designed for personal projects, portfolios, and small organizations. It is not suitable for high-traffic commercial sites. We have fair use policies on CPU and daily hit usage to ensure the platform is stable for everyone. Prohibited usage will be terminated (See Byet's Terms Of Services). Also our hosting have limited daily hit usage, and it does not support email, PHP version selection, DNS management, remote MySQL server and Python/Node.JS

Can I use my own custom domain name?

+

Absolutely! While we provide a free subdomain (based on your chosen username and subdomain), you can easily point your own custom domain (e.g., `yourwebsite.com`) to your hosting account with us.

To do this, please set your domain nameservers at your domain registrar to ours:
ns1.lightnode.qzz.io
ns2.lightnode.qzz.io

After setting the nameservers and your account is active, you can add your custom domain via the 'Addon Domain' section in your hosting control panel. Further instructions are available in our control panel.
